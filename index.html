What is Yuumeno Community?

Welcome to Yuumeno, an entertainment community rooted in the vibrant worlds of anime, gaming, music, manga, VTubers, and Japanese culture. We started our journey in January 2024, establishing our home within the TOPIA karaoke and streaming app. Explore and immerse yourself in the diverse and exciting realms we offer.

What are we trying to achieve?

At Yuumeno, we're dedicated to making every member's dream a reality through exciting projects! Together, we'll create unforgettable memories and push our limits to achieve each member's unique aspirations.
